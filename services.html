Entrepreneurship

Dr. Zhiheng Zhao has served as the Co-founder and CEO of Gantan Limited (Hong Kong) since 2016 and Gantan Limited (Wuxi) since 2026 (www.gantanmark.com). The company was successfully selected as an Incubatee of the Cyberport Incubation Program in 2017, receiving a grant of HK$330,000. In addition, Gantan Limited (Hangzhou) secured funding of CNY ¥4,000,000 through the Hangzhou Qingshan Lake Innovation Project. Gantan's customers include Huawei Technologies Ltd, Hong Kong Air Cargo Terminals Ltd, Lenovo Group Ltd, DKSH Management Ltd, and Gree Electric Appliances Inc., among others. One Chinese patent (ZL201911070277.0) has been granted and subsequently transferred, generating a transfer value of CNY ¥60,000.
